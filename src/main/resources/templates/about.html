About Skateboard Professionals

Skateboard Professionals was created by Jordan Nitta, who wanted to establish the number one skateboard shop in Hawaii

With a commitment to quality, authenticity, and innovation, we aim to be the go-to destination for everything skateboarding in Hawaii. From high-performance decks and precision trucks to stylish apparel and cutting-edge accessories, we curate a meticulously selected range of products to cater to every skater's needs and preferences.

But beyond just selling gear, we aspire to create a welcoming space where skaters can connect, share their passion, and inspire each other to push their boundaries. Whether you're a seasoned pro, an aspiring shredder, or a curious beginner, our doors are open to you.

Together, let's carve out a new era of skateboarding excellence in the Hawaiian islands. Join us on this thrilling journey as we chase our dream of becoming the number one skateboard shop in Hawaii.
